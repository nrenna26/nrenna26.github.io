What was the cultural impact of cybenetics? Cybernetics saw human beings not as individuals, but as components in systems. It was a computer's perspective of the world and reduced human beings to just parts of a system. Humans and machines could be one in the same. One of the founding similarities is that humans and machines both function through feedback loops. If we take Howard Odum's ideas and draw ecosystems as energy circuits, we can see how the energy flows from one organism to the other. The Odum brothers simplified the natural world and created a scientific description of reality. In this experiement, the brothers didn't account for the unpredictability and randomness of the natural world. This fusion of cybernetics and ecology started a new organizing principle for human society as well. There was a new vision for the ordinary world that mirrored how order was created in nature. People should see themselves as equal members in the system, not further defined by hierarchy, careers, education etc. Should the world be seen as one giant spaceship? De-emphasizing the importance of the human being to help the welfare of all humans.
