The milkmaid & her eggs

Patty the Milkmaid was going to market carrying her milk in a pail on her head

As she went along she began calculating what she would do with the money she would get for the milk.

"I'll buy some chickens from Farmer Brown," she said. "And they will lay a dozen eggs each morning”,

“I will sell all those eggs to the preacher’s wife.

“With the money that I get from the sale of these eggs, I'll buy myself a new dimity frock and a chip hat”

“and when I go to market, won't all the young men come up and speak to me!”

Polly Shaw will be that jealous; but I don't care.

I shall just look at her and toss my head like this."

As she spoke she tossed her head back...

the Pail fell off it...

and all the milk was spilt.

So she had to go home and tell her mother what had occurred.

"Ah,my child," said the mother: "Do not count your chickens before they are hatched"
